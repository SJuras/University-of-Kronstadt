Courses We Offer

Choose from our three levels of courses in our curriculum.

Intermediate

Get the basic knowledge foundation or get your knowledge and skills to a higher level with our Intermediate level courses designed to get you started on your learning journey to become the expert in your desired field. Courses span from a single semester to four semesters, depending on the field of your choice. Enroll now and start your learning path with us.

Degree

Get your University level degree in your desired field. This program encompasses full 10 semester University degree level education, recognized internationally. You will receive knowledge and full skillset to become the expert in your desired field, ready to enter the job market. If you choose so, you may study abroad in one of our facilities. The quality of received education and degree recognition does not differentiate upon the location of your studies.

Post-graduation

Take your studies to the next level and receive your Ph.D. Our University offers post-degree education on all fields of studies and in all of our facilities around the world. Your chance to study under the mentorship of world-renowned expert in the field of your choosing. Internships and working practise in world's most prestigious scientific facilities and laboratories. Your chance to be a part of the cutting-edge technology development.

Our Facilities

Each facility meets the highest educational and living standards.

World Class Library

Our library offers vast variety of textbooks and literature, ranging from newest editions to historic, original Renaissance books. Use our highly organized book database system or ask our competent staff to find what you are looking for.

Largest Playgrounds

We offer numerous sports and recreational activities within our newly renovated and modernized sports facilities. Feel free to use the at any time during your stay in our campus. You are welcome to participate in our various regular sports tournaments.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Our cantinas offer meals to the highest of nutritional standards, with various menus tailored to choices of our diverse student population. All nutritional requirements have been met regarding our students cultural backgrounds, beliefs and health conditions.
